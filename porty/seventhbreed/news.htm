Hi and welcome to our web site. Our last gig was at the Stilbay, Freedom Biker Rally ((1000 bikers)) not really biker music, but it was fun. IMPORTANT news, we have decided on a music style change, which has resulted in a line up change, Ziggy has left the band and Werner Beukes has joined. The music has changed to a funkier groovier sound - no moshing just grooving. Don't worry its not slow - its fast groovy beats with running baselines - the guitar has been toned down to include ripping verses and pop choruses - very exciting.
